Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide an estimate?

We typically provide estimates within 24-48 hours of your initial contact. For complex projects, we may schedule an on-site consultation within 3-5 business days.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including weekly lawn care, seasonal cleanup, pruning, fertilization, and irrigation system maintenance.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. EPK Landscaping is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance for your protection and peace of mind.

What's the best time to start a landscaping project?

Spring and fall are ideal for planting, but we work year-round. Hardscaping projects can be done in any season, and we'll advise you on the optimal timing.
